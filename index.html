Nida's Birthday Game Arcade

A collection of little games from toprak

Elemental Bending Practice

Drag the appearing elemental symbols (Water, Earth, Fire, Air) to their correct quadrants. Show your Avatar skills! Sort 10 symbols.

Sunflower Bloom

Tap to plant seeds and watch geometric sunflowers bloom. Fill the garden with 15 beautiful blooms!

Cabbage Cart Chaos

"MY CABBAGES!" Help the Cabbage Merchant! Drag cabbages to his stall, dodging Aang and friends. Deliver 10 cabbages.

Spirit World Path

Connect the glowing spirit nodes in sequence by drawing a path. Don't cross your line! Complete 3 paths to meditate successfully.

Appa's Sky Journey

Yip yip! Drag Appa to navigate the skies, dodge Fire Nation ships, and collect Sky Biscuits. Survive for 60s or get 15 biscuits!

Momo Fruit Catch

Help Momo grab the falling fruit by dragging him around. Catch 10 before missing 3!
